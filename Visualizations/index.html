Impact of Latitude on Weather

Summary

In this project we wanted to take a look at some weather data to see what impacts latitude had on weather. In particular, we will be looking at the following observations:

  • The relationship between the Max Observed Temperature and Latitude for cities located in the Northern Hemisphere
  • The relationship between the Max Observed Temperature and Latitude for cities located in the Southern Hemisphere
  • The relationship between Humidity and Latitude for cities located in the Northern Hemisphere
  • The relationship between Humidity and Latitude for cities located in the Southern Hemisphere

For this analysis, we generated a random list of over 500 cities and pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API.

Once we analyzed the data, we used Matplotlib to vizualize the relationships between the various aspects of weather and a citie's latitude. This site provides the source data and the vizualization we created to display these relationships. Additionally, we have included explanations and descriptions of any trends we observed.
